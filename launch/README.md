# CxOPack — Launch Kit

All copy-paste-ready assets for the public launch of CxOPack Edition I.

## Files

- [`demo-video-script.md`](./demo-video-script.md) — 3-minute walkthrough script + shot list
- [`linkedin-launch.md`](./linkedin-launch.md) — launch-day LinkedIn post (~1,100 chars)
- [`x-thread.md`](./x-thread.md) — 10-tweet X (Twitter) thread
- [`show-hn.md`](./show-hn.md) — Show HN title, body, and first-comment FAQ
- [`indie-hackers.md`](./indie-hackers.md) — Indie Hackers post + community angle
- [`email-sequence-launch-100.md`](./email-sequence-launch-100.md) — 4-email sequence for the first 10 Launch-100 beta customers
- [`schedule.md`](./schedule.md) — launch-week block-by-block plan

## Principles across every asset

- **Specific beats inspirational.** Numbers, times, verbs.
- **One CTA per asset.** Usually the same: `cxopack.vercel.app` (or `cxopack.com` once purchased).
- **Dogfood angle is earned, not claimed.** Show what the product did, don't tell them it's "founder-built."
- **Hooks do 80% of the work.** Spend 80% of the time on the first line.
- **Follow up is mandatory.** Day 2, 4, 7 follow-up content converts the skim-past crowd on their second pass.

## Order of operations on launch day

1. **07:00 Pacific** — Show HN goes live (Tuesday is optimal)
2. **07:15** — X thread publishes, quote-tweeting the Show HN link
3. **09:00 EU** — LinkedIn post publishes with demo GIF
4. **10:00 Pacific** — Email 1 of sequence sends to waitlist (first 30 addresses)
5. **Afternoon** — Indie Hackers post, scheduled for the IH morning bump
6. **Every hour for 6 hours** — answer every HN comment, every X reply, every LinkedIn comment
7. **Day +2** — "Launch day by the numbers" follow-up post on X + LinkedIn
8. **Day +7** — "What I got wrong" follow-up + first customer story
