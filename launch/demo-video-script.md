# Demo video — 3-minute walkthrough

**Goal:** show Donna routing a real decision across 5 executives in Claude Code, end-to-end.
**Target length:** 2:30–3:15.
**Format:** screen recording with voice-over. No face-cam. No intro animation.

## Shot list

### Scene 1 — Hook (0:00–0:10)
**Screen:** split terminal + Notion with the startup's cash flow open.
**VO (direct, peer tone):**
> "Every solo founder runs into the same problem. Every decision needs a CEO, a CTO, a CFO, a head of sales, and a head of marketing. You have one of those people. Here's what I built to have the other four."

### Scene 2 — The landing (0:10–0:30)
**Screen:** scroll through cxopack.com landing — hero, the Board visual, pricing.
**VO:**
> "CxOPack is five AI executive kits you install in Claude, ChatGPT, or Cursor. They're not chatbots — they're structured workflows. And they come with a Chief of Staff called Donna who routes your questions to the right executive and synthesizes one answer."

### Scene 3 — The problem (0:30–0:50)
**Screen:** Claude Code, empty chat.
**VO:**
> "Here's a real question I had this morning: should I raise prices? This touches strategy, finance, sales, and marketing. I'd normally spend an hour spinning on it. Watch what happens instead."

### Scene 4 — The routing (0:50–1:30)
**Screen:** type `/founder Should I raise prices?` and hit Enter.
- Donna thinks for 2 seconds.
- Console output: "Routing to CEO, CFO, Sales, CMO based on your priorities and metrics."
**VO (over the routing):**
> "Donna reads my founder-log — my current priorities, last month's metrics, recent decisions — and decides which executives should weigh in. Here, it's four of the five."

### Scene 5 — The takes (1:30–2:15)
**Screen:** Each persona returns a 2-sentence take. Highlight each as it streams in.
**VO (calm, reading over):**
> "CEO says it's the highest-leverage move I can make, with a written guardrail. CFO models conversion drop, ARPU lift, and sets a kill criterion. Sales tells me most demos already treat my price as low. CMO warns that a silent price raise reads as greed — pair it with a proof point."

### Scene 6 — The synthesis (2:15–2:40)
**Screen:** Donna's final recommendation panel — one paragraph, signature "Decide today."
**VO:**
> "Donna synthesizes a single recommendation. One paragraph. Named rollback criteria. Signs it 'Decide today.' Total time: thirty seconds. The decision is logged. I move on."

### Scene 7 — Outro (2:40–3:00)
**Screen:** cxopack.com pricing page.
**VO:**
> "Fifty euros for a single kit. A hundred and forty-nine a year for the full suite plus Donna. First hundred members lock in ninety-nine forever. Link's in the description. I'll be in the comments."
