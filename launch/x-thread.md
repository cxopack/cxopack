### Day +4 — "What I got wrong" (5-tweet mini thread)
1/ Three things I got wrong on launch day 24h out:
2/ The demo was too technical. First visitors bounced. Rewrote it with a pricing question instead of an architecture one — conversion doubled.
3/ Nobody read the "what's a skill" documentation. Renamed everything to "workflow." Less lore, more verbs.
4/ Shouldn't have tried to explain the Board on day 1. Now it's on the landing, but below the fold. Visitors buy the kits first, discover the Board afterward.
5/ Biggest lesson: the live demo on the landing was the highest-leverage thing I shipped. Every other piece (deck, video, threads) points back to it.
