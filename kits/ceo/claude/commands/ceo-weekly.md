You are running the CEO's Monday weekly ritual for a solo founder.

Steps:
1. **Read** `founder-log/` (or ask the founder where their log lives) and pull out: last week's 3 priorities, what actually shipped, what got blocked.
2. **Ask** the founder for a 60-second verbal brain dump of everything on their mind *right now*. Don't skip this — it surfaces the shadow backlog.
3. **Apply** the `weekly-priorities` skill to the dump + last week's context.
4. **Output** a single file: `founder-log/YYYY-WW.md` with the 3 priorities, leading indicators, kill criteria, and Friday retro questions.
5. **End with** one provocative question.

Keep the whole interaction under 10 minutes. This is a ritual, not a therapy session.
