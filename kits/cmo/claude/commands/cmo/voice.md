Run the **brand-voice** skill. Extract voice from existing content (don't invent):

1. Collect 5 proud artifacts + 2 off-brand ones.
2. Analyze patterns: sentence length, humor, formality, self-reference, signature phrases.
3. 5 rules (positive), 5 anti-rules (negative), 10 before/after examples across contexts (hero, FAQ, cold email, tweet, LI, newsletter, update, testimonial ask, rebuttal, OOO).

Reject "authentic and confident" — rules must be specific. Save to `content/brand-voice.md`. This doc is referenced by every other content tool + contract.
