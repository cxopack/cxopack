---
description: Weekly 15-minute pipeline review. Kill deals >45d with no movement. No mercy.
---

Run the **pipeline-review** skill. Friday ritual:

1. Read last week's pipeline review.
2. Per open deal (60 sec each): stage change with evidence? Next step + date? Blocker?
3. Kill list: any deal failing the 3 rules (no next step, >45d no movement, stage w/o evidence).
4. Next week focus: 3 deals only (close probability × deal size).
5. Leading indicators count: cold emails, discovery calls, demos, proposals, closed won, churn.

Save to `sales/pipeline/YYYY-WW.md`. Be brutal — small pipelines die of tolerated dead weight.
