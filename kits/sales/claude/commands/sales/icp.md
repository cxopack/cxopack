description: Define the ICP via 5-question interview — who you refuse, not just who you accept.
---

Run the **icp-workshop** skill. Ask the 5 questions one at a time, refuse to move on until each is concretely answered:

1. 3 best customers, what they have in common (not paying most — most successful)
2. Trigger event in the 30 days before they bought
3. Their alternative today (real workaround, not a competitor logo)
4. Willingness-to-pay anchor (what they already pay for adjacent tools)
5. Disqualifiers (customers who churned, what they shared)

Force hard disqualifiers. Refuse three-way ICPs — rank and pick one for the next 90 days. Save to `sales/icp.md`.
